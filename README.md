# Tunneler TNT

**Tunneler TNT** is a vanilla-friendly expansion mod for Minecraft (Fabric) that adds specialized explosives to your arsenal.
Designed to fit seamlessly into the base game, these new TNT types offer powerful utility for mining and excavation without feeling out of place.

## üß® Features

### 1. Heavy TNT
A dense, high-power explosive designed for massive excavation.
* **Explosion Power:** 7.0 (nearly double vanilla TNT).
* **Fuse Time:** 6 Seconds (120 ticks) ‚Äî gives you extra time to clear the blast zone.
* **Behavior:** Works exactly like standard TNT, just bigger and louder.

### 2. Tunneler TNT
A specialized directional charge used for rapid mining and breaching.
* **Directional:** Carves a tunnel *forward* based on the direction it was facing when placed.
* **Dimensions:** Creates a 16-block long tunnel with a radius of 4 blocks.
* **Smart Mining:** * **Ore Safety:** Automatically detects and *ignores* precious blocks (Ores, Ancient Debris, Spawners, Chests), leaving them floating safely in the air.
    * **Natural Look:** Uses a noise algorithm to create rough, cave-like walls instead of perfect mathematical cylinders.

**Progression:**
The mod integrates naturally with survival progression.
* Recipes are **hidden** by default.
* **Unlock Condition:** Simply obtain a block of **Vanilla TNT** to automatically unlock the recipes for both Heavy and Tunneler TNT in your recipe book.

**Crafting:**
* **Heavy TNT:** Reinforced with extra Gunpowder (Gunpowder - TNT - Gunpowder).
* **Tunneler TNT:** Directed using Iron Nuggets and Gunpowder.
